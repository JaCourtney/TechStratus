Frequently Asked Questions

Do you come to my home, or can you help remotely?

Both. Many problems can be fixed remotely, which is faster and more convenient. If needed, we can also schedule an in-home visit.

What kinds of problems can you help with?

We help with everyday technology issues. Things like:

  • Slow computers
  • Wi-Fi and internet problems
  • Device setup
  • Password and email help
  • Online safety
  • Home office support

If you're not sure, just ask.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes. Many clients choose monthly support so they always have someone to call when something comes up. It's a simple way to prevent problems and get help quickly.

Is my information private and secure?

Absolutely. We take privacy seriously and treat your information with care. We never share personal data and always follow secure practices.
